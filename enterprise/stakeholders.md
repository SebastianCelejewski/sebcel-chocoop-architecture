# Stakeholders

## Household Users

Primary users of the system responsible for daily task management and cooperation.

### Concerns
- simplicity
- responsiveness
- fairness
- visibility of contributions

## System Creator and Operator

Responsible for architecture, implementation, deployment, and operations.

### Concerns
- simplicity
- maintainability
- operational overhead
- scalability
- incremental evolution
- encouraging household participation and cooperation
- supporting fair distribution of household responsibilities
- reinforcing positive behavioral patterns within the family

## Future Contributors

Potential future developers or collaborators.

### Concerns
- understandability
- documentation quality
- architectural consistency
