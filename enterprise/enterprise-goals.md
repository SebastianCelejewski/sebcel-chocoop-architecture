# Enterprise Goals

Project: Celejewski Family Enterprise
Scope: Preliminary Phase / Business Architecture
Status: Draft
Last Updated: 2026-06-05
Owner: Sebastian Celejewski

## Household Goals

* maintain household order
* distribute household responsibilities fairly
* improve visibility of household contributions
* encourage active participation in household work
* reduce household coordination friction
* strengthen cooperative household behavior
* improve awareness of pending and completed work

## Social and Emotional Goals

* preserve a positive family atmosphere
* encourage appreciation and recognition of contributions
* avoid excessive conflict around household responsibilities
* support constructive communication regarding fairness and participation
* make household work more visible, acknowledged, and socially appreciated

## Supporting Technical and Personal Goals

* support experimentation and learning
* develop software engineering and cloud architecture skills
* maintain low operational overhead
* evolve systems incrementally
* use the enterprise ecosystem as a practical learning environment
* support creative and recreational software development activities
